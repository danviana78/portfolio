/ TASTE of São Paulo

SÃO PAULO, BRAZIL, 2017.

Thirteen years ago, curious and passionate about good food had a different experience in London. Carried out in an unusual space mounted outdoors, the Taste Festivals were born to provide a meeting with renowned chefs, unique lunches and dinners, participation in exclusive classes and lots of fun all in one place.

The world’s best dining festival is staged every year in twenty cities across different countries and it landed in Brazil for its debut in September 2016 and second edition in August 2017. Thirty seven thousand lovers of good food indulged themselves in thirty great restaurants from São Paulo, and did cooking classes, attended seminars and demonstrations run by more than 60 chefs, and enjoyed a range of entertainment options in a welcoming atmosphere.

The challenge. The inspiration.

In 2017, we were living in an age of opposites. A world divided in two sides and the same was happening in Brazil. But São Paulo is a city that welcomes people, a melting pot that has the history of mixing everyone together.

Great São Paulo has 21 millions inhabitants, 51 international cuisines represented in the city and is the third biggest italian city in the world, the biggest japanese city outside Japan, third biggest lebanese city outside Lebanon, the biggest portuguese city outside Portugal and the biggest spanish city outside Spain.

However, all this mixture needed something to bond and it is through food that we united. Afterall, São Paulo is the Worldwide Gastronomy Capital. Different cuisines, different styles, all mixed together in the Taste of São Paulo to celebrate this mixture and spread the message.

Celebrate. Mix. Discover. Get together.
