/ HELLO, my name is Daniel.

From an early age, I have unconsciously discovered that in the creative industry, all branches are connected. At that time, I did not know what the meaning of it was, I just knew that when I was skateboarding, listening to music in my walkman, playing basketball and producing small punk rock concerts, the visual connection would be the sense that would complete my personal experiences, my personal expression. It did not take me long to realize that "design" was the path to be followed, it was the natural choice. I have thus graduated as a Graphic and Product Designer and later enhanced my strategic and analytic point of view, with a Strategic Marketing MBA.

Along the way, I have gained professional experience at design offices, running my own design business and being an active part of the growth of a small company into a main industry player, from which I became the creative department leader, always fostering teamwork and criptical thinking.

Today, after a 15+ years career, 10 of which working in the international sports and entertainment industry, I see Design and Marketing as tools that can help provide people the best ways to connect to experiences that are relevant to themselves. Looking foward, my goal is to pursue my continuous professional growth through the work in innovative and authentic projects that create value for brands and their audience.
