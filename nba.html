/ NBA Global Games

RIO DE JANEIRO, BRAZIL, 2015.

The NBA Global Games are a series of games featuring NBA teams that are played outside the United States and Canada. Its purpose is to bring teams from the NBA to play games against either another NBA team or a foreign club.

Passion in court.

The third edition of the NBA Global Games in Brazil, was the first one with a brazilian team facing a NBA team. Orlando Magic had to measure strenght with Flamengo, the biggest legion of fans in brazilian soccer.

From a styleguide created by NBA headquarters we developed all the arena look & feel and the diverse applications ranging from media, signage, press kit to pre-game press conference.
